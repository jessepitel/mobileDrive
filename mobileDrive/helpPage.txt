To change directories:
    1) Tap once on any directory

To cange to a previouse directory in current path:
    1) Scroll over to the directory you want in the "Path" field at the top
    2) Tap once on the directory that directory

To go back:
    1) Tap once on the "<" button

To add a directory:
    1) Tap once on the add directory button
    2) Enter in a name
    3) Tap "OK"
    
To see a file's/directoie's meta data:
    1) Tap and hold any file/directory
    
To rename a file/directory:
    1) Tap and hold the file/directory
    2) Tap the rename button
    3) Enter a new name
    4) Tap "OK"
    
To move a file/directory:
    1) Tap and hold the file/directory
    2) Tap the move button
    3) Enter a new path
    4) Tap "OK"
    
To delete a file/directory:
    1) Tap and hold the file/directory
    2) Tap the delete button
    3) Tap "OK"
